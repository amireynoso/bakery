Melitas

By Ami Reynoso
January 29, 2022

Melitas are very popular honey cookies from Argentina. I couldn't find anything similar in Canada, so I started making them myself. I started off with Matias Chavero's recipe and adapted it slightly to my tasting. They are, dare I say, even better than the original cookies.

This recipe yields about 20 cookies, and they freeze beautifully.
